About TrashFire Games

Our Story

Trashfire Games, created in 2022, is a start-up video game studio designed to help professionals learn more about the gaming industry by participating in the production of a game under Trashfire.

Building portfolio work and learning new skills within the members' specialized field, Trashfire consists of four departments: Art, Programming, Audio, and Marketing.

We're a tiny, weird, and deeply passionate indie team who just released our first game, An Eternity Gone By. We make games about messy feelings, meaningful friendships, and magical nonsense — and this one means the world to us.

Our Philosophy

At Trashfire, we believe in telling stories that stick with you. We value sincerity over polish, voice over volume, and building things with love (and probably too much coffee).

An Eternity Gone By

Game Overview

An Eternity Gone By is a cozy, chaotic hack-and-slash where you play as Mirai, a stubborn telekinetic girl on a mission to save her best friend.

Everything in the game is an object: You hurl chairs and apples and broomsticks at absurd monsters in a dreamlike world — all while navigating a time-looping story about grief, growth, and doing what's right to save your best friend.
